BUNDA SEGALA BANGSA

Bunda Segala Bangsa, also known as Mother of All Nations in English, is a statue of Mother Mary in Nilo hill, 5 km south-west from Maumere. The statue stands 18 metres (59 ft) tall. With its pedestal and foundation, however, it is 28 metres (92 ft) tall. The statue was constructed on 2005. It is 6-ton and is clad in copper. It is easily the tallest statue raised in Sikka Regency.

On certain day, especially Catholic holidays, local Catholic community helds a ritual called Tuan Ma. The ritual is held to welcome a woman who is believed to be Mother Mary. The locals around the statue claim that it is true a figure of Virgin Mary is often seen.

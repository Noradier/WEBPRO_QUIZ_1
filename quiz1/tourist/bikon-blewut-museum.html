BIKON BLEWUT MUSEUM

Bikon Blewut Museum is located on the hills of Nita District in Sikka Regency. The museum holds several collections of cultural tools from old stone age to bronze age. In addition, this museum also stores fauna and flora fossils as well as collection of cultural arts from people of Flores in contemporary era. The collection were collected starting in 1965 by Father Verhoeven, a missionary of the SVD Order.

There are some unique collections in the museum. Among them is an ancient elephant fossils. The elephant fossils are consisted of elephant ivory and teeth. This discovery estimates that elephant once lived on Flores when "the land was still together".

Beside the elephant fossils, the museum also has what is believed to be early human fossils from Flores. Aside from fossils, the museum's unique collection includes Dongson's Keris, which is believed to be originated from Northern Vietnam, an art collection of General Ahmad Yani, a revolution hero from Indonesia, and an art collection of Rendo, a female figure representing Portugese.

Rumpu Rampe

Rumbu Rampe is a local food in Nusa Tenggara Timur.

This dish is made of papaya flowers, young papapya fruit, and banana hearts. It is also mixed with leaves like moringa leaves, cassava leaves and papaya leaves. Nowadays, this dish is often combined with kale, shrimp and anchovies.

Usually, Rumpu Rampe is eaten together with Jagung Bose, a traditional food from Nusa Tenggara Timur.

MAUMERE

My hometown is Maumere in Flores Island.

Maumere is the seat capital of Sikka Regency in Nusa Tenggara Timur Province. It is also the largest city in Flores.

It has an airport and harbor, Frans Xavier Seda Airport or more commonly called Frans Seda, and Laurentius Say Port. There is also status of Mother Mary in Nilo Hill, the tallest statue in Sikka. Maumere also has beautiful beaches which attract tourists from all around the world.
